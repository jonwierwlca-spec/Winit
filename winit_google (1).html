📖 Where to find these numbers in LoseIt ﹢
Why it matters Daily calories bounce around — one rough day isn't failure. What predicts weight loss is your total net calories across the whole week. That's the number WinIt tracks.
How to find it in LoseIt
1 Open LoseIt and tap the Log tab at the bottom
2 Tap the Under number in the circle at the top
3 Look for "Calories under budget" at the bottom of the breakdown — that's your number. It already includes any exercise calories.
💡 Tip: Enter yesterday's number each morning before you start logging today. Takes 10 seconds.

What's working 🔍
Based on your actual logged data — calorie ranges, net cals, and how your weight responded the next day.
Calories eaten → next-day weight change
Average weight change the morning after days in each calorie range. Negative = lost. Too low can cause retention.
Net cals → next-day weight change
Net cals = calories eaten minus exercise burn. The number that actually drives fat loss.
Under budget → next-day weight change
Your "calories under budget" from LoseIt. Higher = more deficit, but the sweet spot matters.
Possible retention days
Days where calories were unusually low (<1000) but weight went UP the next morning — a classic signal of stress-response retention or water fluctuation.
